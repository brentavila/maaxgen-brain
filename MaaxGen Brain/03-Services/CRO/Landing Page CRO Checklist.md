# Landing Page CRO Checklist

## First Fold

- Is the offer clear within 5 seconds?
- Is the headline specific?
- Is there one primary CTA?
- Is the CTA above the fold?
- Is there a trust signal above the fold?
- Is the page visually simple?
- Is the image relevant?
- Is there too much text?

## Offer

- Is the offer easy to understand?
- Is there urgency?
- Is there value?
- Is there risk reversal?
- Is pricing clear when appropriate?

## Trust

- Reviews
- Testimonials
- Years in business
- Certifications
- Guarantees
- Before/after examples
- Case studies
- Badges

## Friction

- Too many form fields?
- Slow load time?
- Confusing CTA?
- Weak mobile layout?
- Poor image quality?
- No phone number?
- No location info?
- No reason to act now?

## Conversion Actions

- Call
- Form
- Quote request
- Book online
- Purchase
- Chat
- SMS
