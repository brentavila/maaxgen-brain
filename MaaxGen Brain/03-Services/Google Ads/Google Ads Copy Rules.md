# Google Ads Copy Rules

## Brent’s Preferred Google Ads Format

When creating Google Ads copy:

- Provide 10 headlines
- Each headline must be 30 characters or fewer including spaces
- Use Start Case
- Include the keyword when possible
- Include a call to action
- Do not use exclamation points
- Provide long headlines up to 90 characters when requested
- Provide descriptions when requested
- Make copy specific to the offer, location, and service

## Headline Style

Good:

- Book Auto Repair
- Subaru Repair Near Me
- Schedule Service Today
- Get A Free Quote
- Trusted Local Mechanics

Bad:

- Best Service Ever!!!
- We Are The #1 Company
- Click Here Now
- Generic Marketing Copy

## Ad Copy Priorities

1. Match the search intent
2. Mention the service
3. Mention the location when helpful
4. Include the offer
5. Include a clear CTA
6. Avoid vague claims
7. Avoid overhyped language
