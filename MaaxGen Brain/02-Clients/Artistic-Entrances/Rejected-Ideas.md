# Artistic Entrances Rejected Ideas

## Current Constraints

These items are out of scope or unsafe for the door configurator unless the owners explicitly approve a future change.

### Accepting Custom-Door Payments In Version 1

**Decision:** Rejected for version 1.

**Reason:** The experience is focused on qualified lead generation. Measurements, option compatibility, delivery, installation, and final pricing require human confirmation.

### Storing Product Images In MaaxGen Brain

**Decision:** Rejected.

**Reason:** Hundreds of large binary files would bloat Git history and Obsidian sync. Product media belongs in local staging and a production CDN.

### Claiming Every Combination Is Photorealistic

**Decision:** Rejected for version 1.

**Reason:** Individual layered assets do not yet exist for every handle, glass, finish, and door combination. Version 1 uses a hybrid preview and an explicit selection summary.

### Automatic Discounts From The AI Bot

**Decision:** Rejected.

**Reason:** Meeting decisions require discount guardrails and human escalation. The configurator does not calculate or issue discounts.

### Universal White-Glove Or Expedited Delivery Claims

**Decision:** Rejected.

**Reason:** Availability varies by project and location. The sales team must confirm delivery, installation, and timing.
