# BGW Doors Merchant Center

## Purpose

Merchant Center is a major driver of BGW Doors Google Ads performance because Performance Max Shopping depends heavily on product feed quality, product data accuracy, product images, product pricing, and product eligibility.

The goal of this file is to document Merchant Center health, product feed issues, product listing improvements, pricing competitiveness, and opportunities to improve Shopping/PMax performance.

Google’s Merchant Center product data specification includes important fields like price and availability, and Google says these attributes are shown to potential customers in ads and free listings. Merchant Center Analytics also includes pricing insights, including price benchmarks and sale price suggestions. Google also provides “Needs attention” reporting to prioritize product issues that may affect performance. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

# Main Goal

Improve Merchant Center quality so BGW Doors products are more eligible, more competitive, more clickable, and more likely to convert through Google Ads and free listings.

# Product Listing Quality

## Product Title Review

Review whether product titles include important purchase-intent details.

Potential title elements:

- Material
- Door style
- Door type
- Size
- Single/double
- Sidelights
- Exterior/front door wording
- Finish/color
- In-stock/custom
- Brand if useful

## Product Title Questions

- Are titles too vague?
- Do titles include “front door” or “exterior door” when relevant?
- Do titles include material such as wood, fiberglass, or iron?
- Do titles include style or model names?
- Do titles include important dimensions?
- Do titles match how buyers search?
- Are titles overstuffed or unnatural?
- Are best-selling products titled better than poor-performing products?

## Product Description Review

Check whether descriptions explain:

- Material
- Size/dimensions
- Best use case
- Style
- Included components
- Shipping expectations
- Warranty if applicable
- Installation considerations
- Customization options
- Trust/reassurance

## Product Image Review

Check:

- Main image quality
- White background or clean product image
- Lifestyle images
- Close-up detail images
- Door installed images
- Image consistency
- Mobile visibility
- Image approval status
- Whether images help high-ticket buyers trust the product

# Custom Labels

## Recommended Custom Label Ideas

Use custom labels to help analyze and segment products.

Possible labels:

- Material: wood, fiberglass, iron
- Door Type: single, double, sidelight
- Inventory Status: in-stock, custom, closeout
- Margin Tier: high, medium, low
- Price Tier: premium, mid, entry
- Performance Tier: hero, stable, test, poor
- Shipping Eligibility: flat-rate, quote-required, pickup-only
- Seasonality: evergreen, seasonal
- Priority: push, monitor, limit, exclude

## Custom Label Questions

- Are custom labels currently used?
- Are products grouped in a way that supports PMax strategy?
- Can custom labels help isolate winners and losers?
- Can custom labels help prevent poor products from consuming budget?
- Can custom labels help create better asset groups or listing groups?
