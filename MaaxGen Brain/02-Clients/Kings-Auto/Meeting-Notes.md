# King's Auto Meeting Notes

Google Meet generates a meeting summary in Google Drive (Google Doc). Until Drive automation is connected, import notes manually and process them here so they feed task lists, [[Change-Log]], [[Client-Brief]], and recommendations.

## Manual import (now)

1. Open the Meet notes Google Doc in Drive.
2. **File → Download → Plain text (.txt)**.
3. Save to `Data/Meetings/YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.txt` in this client folder.
4. In Cursor, ask: *"Process the King's Auto meeting notes from Data/Meetings/[filename] into Meeting-Notes.md and update Next-Actions."*

Raw exports live in [[Data/Meetings/Raw]].
